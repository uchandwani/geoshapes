Su-Art Learning Aids has conceptualised, designed the innovative Mathematics apparatuses for the school’s existing Math lab. Our products are unique and one of its kind. These exciting apparatuses provide the opportunity for the students to explore research, collect data, test, and verify the multiple mathematical theorems and concepts. All these theorems and concepts are mapped to CBSE / ICSE / State board math curriculum.

Apparatus advantage

To students – Students can explore, verify, and test the Math theorems and concepts that are already taught in the class. The suggested system will make the students learning more systemic. The system strongly advocates the students should state the hypothesis, collect data. And finally, test the hypothesis against the collected data. To Teachers – Teachers can carry these apparatuses in the class to demonstrate the Math concepts. To school – Schools can have these apt Mathematical apparatuses in their Math lab. These mathematical apparatuses also contribute effectively to BaLA (Building as Learning Aids)
